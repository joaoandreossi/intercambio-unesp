About UNESP


UNESP is the most successful model of a multicampus university and is focused on nearly every field of experimental and theoretical sciences such as: engineering, health, communications, humanities, social sciences and arts, among others. The university is supported by State funds and offers free public higher education in Sao Paulo State.


Its high qualification level can be confirmed through the expressive number of alumni inserted in the professional market, as well as its top position in Brazilian national ranks. Unesp is a respected institution with accomplished academic staff, fully-accredited programs and strong research base. It is one of the largest and most important Brazilian universities, with distinguished achievements in teaching, research and extension services.


Unesp has 140 international agreements with 28 countries. Students from our partner universities are welcome to participate in our international exchange programs. They receive total support from the International Office and advisors in each campus, who are responsible for giving advice and information on accommodation, local facilities, visas, among other important issues related to their stay in Brazil.
